Windward Mayreau

For big parts of 2021, during the pandemic, I lived and worked on Mayreau in St. Vincent and the Grenadines, with my friend and business partner Kamran, on an eco-resort project called Windward Mayreau.

The project is aiming to create a totally off-grid eco-resort using solar and wind power for electricity, desalination for drinking water, eco-friendly custom-made prefab bungalows, community programs and a lot more!
