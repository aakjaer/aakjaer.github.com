Windward Mayreau

For big parts of 2021 I lived and worked on a tiny island called Mayreau in St. Vincent and the Grenadines, with my friend and business partner Kamran, on an eco-resort project called Windward Mayreau.

In short (which isn't possible), we have taken the first steps of creating a totally off-grid eco-resort using solar power, desalination for drinking water, eco-friendly custom-made prefab bungalows, community programs and a lot more!
