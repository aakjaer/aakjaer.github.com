Project details

I was hired to redesign and reimplement Volkswagen's service-booking form from ground up, to make service bookings smooth for mobile visitors, and improve the total number of online service bookings.

The old sign-up flow was a very traditional, fairly unstyled, corporate looking, and way too many form fields, that I initially had to convince the client to optimize. My idea was to challange the existing somewhat conservative and restricted CI that Volkswagen is infamous for, while maintaining a calm and professional look/feel. Everything was build with a Jekyll/Bootstrap prototype with a trace of JS.
